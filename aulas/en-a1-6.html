Section 2 of 5
Grammar

1. TO BE — Review

Affirmative

He is my brother.

She is my sister.

They are my cousins.

Negative

He isn’t my uncle.

She isn’t my aunt.

Questions

Is he your father?

Is she your mother?

2. Possessive ’S

We use ’s to show possession.

Anna’s brother

John’s sister

Emma’s mother

Lucas’s father

3. More examples

Tara’s sister is Lily.

Daniel’s father is a doctor.

Emma’s mother is a teacher.

John’s cousin is Anna.

4. Review — My / Your / His / Her

My sister

Your brother

His father

Her mother
