Dialogues
Dialogue 1 — Family influence
EmmaDo you think family position influences personality?
LucasYes, I think it does. First-born children are often more responsible.
EmmaI agree, but I don’t think that’s always true.
LucasThat’s fair. Some people are more independent, no matter their position.
EmmaExactly. I was the youngest, but I was quite serious.
LucasReally? I thought younger children were usually more relaxed.
EmmaThat’s the stereotype, but it depends on the family.
Dialogue 2 — Talking about the past
MeiI was thinking about my childhood yesterday.
AnaReally? What were you like?
MeiI was quite curious and creative.
AnaThat’s nice.
MeiMy parents always encouraged me to learn new things.
AnaThat probably influenced your personality.
MeiYes, I think so. I had already developed many skills before I finished school.
AnaThat’s impressive.
Dialogue 3 — Life choices
MarkWhen I finished school, I didn’t know what to do.
JelenaThat’s normal. What happened next?
MarkI started working, but I quickly realised that I wanted something different.
JelenaSo what did you do?
MarkI went to university and studied law.
JelenaThat was a good decision.
MarkYes, it helped me develop important skills.
JelenaI think education plays a big role in our lives.

Section 2 of 5
Grammar

1. Past Simple + sequência narrativa

I finished school

I started working

I realised something

2. Past Perfect

I had already developed skills

They had made decisions

3. Linking ideas

but / however / so / because / no matter

I wanted to study, but I didn’t have time

It depends on the family

4. Adjectives from nouns

responsibility → responsible

success → successful

skill → skilful

intellect → intellectual

art → artistic
