Dialogues
Dialogue 1 — A difficult decision
EmmaHave you ever regretted an important decision?
LucasYes, definitely. A few years ago, I turned down a job opportunity abroad.
EmmaReally? Why?
LucasI was afraid of leaving my family and starting over in another country.
EmmaThat’s understandable. Do you regret it now?
LucasSometimes. I think I would have learned a lot from that experience.
EmmaMaybe, but life can change quickly. Another opportunity could appear.
LucasTrue. I try not to think too much about the past anymore.
Dialogue 2 — Taking risks
AnaDo you think people should take more risks in life?
MeiIt depends. Some risks can completely change your future.
AnaThat’s true.
MeiIf I hadn’t started studying English, my life would probably be very different today.
AnaSame here. Learning another language opens many doors.
MeiExactly. I’ve become much more confident since I started speaking with people from other countries.
AnaI think experiences are what help people grow the most.
MeiI completely agree.
Dialogue 3 — Looking back
MarkWhat advice would you give to your younger self?
JelenaHonestly, I’d tell myself not to worry so much about what other people think.
MarkThat’s good advice.
JelenaI used to compare myself to everyone around me.
MarkA lot of people do that.
JelenaYes, but over time I realised everyone follows a different path.
MarkI think people become happier when they stop trying to please everyone.
JelenaDefinitely. Life becomes much lighter after that.
