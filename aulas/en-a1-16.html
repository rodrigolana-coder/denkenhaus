Section 2 of 5
Grammar

1. Position of adjectives

In English, adjectives usually come before the noun.

a big house

a small café

a cheap restaurant

a fast car

a good teacher

a cold drink

a hot coffee

a new phone

2. TO BE + adjective

We also use adjectives after the verb to be.

The café is nice.

The coffee is hot.

The drinks are cold.

My phone is new.

The shop is big.

3. Very / Really / Quite

These words make adjectives stronger or softer.

It’s very good.

It’s really expensive.

It’s quite small.

She’s very friendly.

The food is really good.

4. Review — There is / There are

There is a café.

There is a supermarket.

There are two restaurants.

There are three shops.
