Why Does Big Ben Ring Every Hour?
One of the most famous landmarks in the United Kingdom is Big Ben.
Many people think Big Ben is the name of the clock, but that’s not correct. Big Ben is actually the name of the largest bell inside the tower. The tower itself is now called Elizabeth Tower.
The clock has four faces, one on each side of the tower, so people can see the time from different directions.
Every hour, the bell rings to tell the time. Before each hour, the clock also plays a famous melody called the Westminster Chimes.
Thousands of tourists visit London every day to see Big Ben and take photos.
The clock has become a symbol of London and of the United Kingdom. It is also one of the most photographed landmarks in the world.
If you visit London, you will probably hear Big Ben before you see it!
