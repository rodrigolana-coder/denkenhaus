Section 2 of 5
Grammar Review

1. Past forms

Past Simple

I travelled last year.

Past Continuous

I was studying while she was cooking.

Past Perfect

I had already finished before he arrived.

Used to

We used to feel nervous when speaking English.

2. Present Perfect

Experiences

I’ve visited many places.

Duration

I’ve been studying English for three years.

Recent situations

I’ve been feeling stressed recently.

3. Hypothetical language

would

I’d like to study abroad.

I’d rather work from home.

Second Conditional

If I had more time, I’d travel more.

If I managed my time better, I’d feel less stressed.

4. Main uses

Past forms → storytelling, past habits and life experiences.

Present Perfect → experiences, unfinished time, duration and recent changes.

Hypothetical language → dreams, preferences, imagination and unreal situations.
