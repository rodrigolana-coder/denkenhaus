How to talk about places

A: What’s your favourite place? — B: My favourite place is the beach.

A: What can you see? — B: I can see a river and some mountains.

A: Where is that museum? — B: It’s near the city centre.

A: Would you like to visit this place? — B: Yes, I would.

Section 2 of 5
Grammar

1. This / That — Review

Use this for something near.

Use that for something far.

This beach is beautiful.

That airport is very big.

This museum is interesting.

That market is busy.

2. These / Those — Review

Use these for plural things near.

Use those for plural things far.

These mountains are beautiful.

Those theatres are old.

These photos are great.

Those rivers are clean.

3. Questions

What’s this?

What’s that?

What are these?

What are those?

What’s this building?

What are those mountains?

4. Review — Adjectives

beautiful

old / new

clean
