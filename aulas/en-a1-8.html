How to talk about colors

A: What color is it? — B: It’s blue.

A: What color is your car? — B: It’s white.

A: What color are your eyes? — B: They’re brown.

A: What color is your hair? — B: It’s black.

Grammar

1. Colors with TO BE

Singular

It is blue.

It is red.

My car is white.

Her phone is black.

Plural

They are green.

2. Questions

What color is it?

3. Review — TO BE

I am · You are · He is · She is · It is · We are · They are

I’m Brazilian.

You’re my friend.

He’s a student.

She’s a teacher.

It’s red.

We’re classmates.

They’re cousins.

4. Review — Possessive Adjectives

my · your · his · her · its · our · their

My backpack is blue.

Her car is red.

Their books are green.

Our classroom is big.
