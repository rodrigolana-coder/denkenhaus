Grammar

1. Present Simple — All persons

SubjectAffirmativeNegativeQuestion
II work.I don’t work.Do I work?*
YouYou work.You don’t work.Do you work?
HeHe works.He doesn’t work.Does he work?
SheShe works.She doesn’t work.Does she work?
ItIt works.It doesn’t work.Does it work?
WeWe work.We don’t work.Do we work?
TheyThey work.They don’t work.Do they work?

*"Do I...?" is grammatically correct but much less common than the other forms.

2. Verb changes

Most verbs simply add -s.

work → works

live → lives

read → reads

like → likes

Verbs ending in -sh, -ch, -ss, -x, -o usually add -es.

watch → watches

finish → finishes

wash → washes

go → goes

fix → fixes

Irregular verb:

have → has

3. Questions

Use Do with I, you, we, they.

Do you work?

Do they live here?

Do we have class today?

Use Does with he, she, it.

Does he work?

Does she like coffee?

Does it open at nine?

4. Negative sentences

Use don’t with I, you, we, they.

I don’t work here.

They don’t like tea.

Use doesn’t with he, she, it.

He doesn’t drive.

She doesn’t like football.

It doesn’t open on Sundays.

5. Adverbs of frequency (review)
