Section 2 of 5
Grammar

1. Present Simple

We use the Present Simple to talk about routines, facts and things we like.

I live in London.

You work in a hospital.

We have two children.

They like music.

2. Questions with DO

Do you live here?

Do you work nearby?

Do you have any brothers?

Do you like coffee?

Short answers

Yes, I do.

No, I don’t.

3. Negative sentences

I don’t like coffee.

You don’t work here.

We don’t have a car.

They don’t live in this city.

4. LIKE / LOVE

I like music.

I love films.

I don’t like football.

We like travelling.

She likes coffee. (You’ll study he/she + -s in the next lesson.)

5. Review — Adverbs of frequency

always

usually

often

sometimes

never

I usually read before bed.

We often go to cafés.

They never drink coffee.
