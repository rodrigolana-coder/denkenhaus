Why Is Tea So Important in the UK?
When people think about the United Kingdom, they often think about tea.
British people drink millions of cups of tea every day.
Many people have tea in the morning, in the afternoon, and in the evening.
Tea is more than just a drink.
It is often a reason to stop working, relax and talk with family, friends or colleagues.
Many people ask: "Would you like a cup of tea?"
It is a very common and polite question in Britain.
People usually drink black tea with milk, but some people prefer tea without milk or with lemon.
Tea is popular at home, at work and even on trains.
Many offices have a "tea break" during the day.
Of course, not everyone in Britain drinks tea.
Some people prefer coffee, hot chocolate or other drinks.
However, tea is still one of the country's most famous traditions.
