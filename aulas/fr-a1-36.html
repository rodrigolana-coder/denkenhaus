MeiWas it very different?

MeiI have never used one, but I have seen them in museums.

Gramática

1. Present Perfect

have / has + past participle

I have watched

She has learnt

2. Affirmative

I have watched that programme.

He has become popular.

3. Negative

I haven’t watched it yet.

She hasn’t seen that presenter.

4. Questions

Have you watched that programme?

Has he used that television?

5. Key words

already / never / yet
