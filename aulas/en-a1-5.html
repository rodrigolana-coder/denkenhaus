Why is English spoken in so many countries?
English is one of the most popular languages in the world.
Today, people speak English in many countries, including the United Kingdom, the United States, Canada, Australia and New Zealand.
A long time ago, people from Britain travelled to different parts of the world. Because of this, English became the main language in many places.
Now, millions of people learn English at school. Many students study English because they want to travel, watch films, listen to music or talk to people from other countries.
English is also very common on the internet. Many websites, videos and apps use English.
One interesting fact is that English has words from many different languages. For example, some English words come from French, German, Italian and other languages.
Today, English helps people communicate around the world. Even when two people are from different countries, they often use English to talk to each other.
