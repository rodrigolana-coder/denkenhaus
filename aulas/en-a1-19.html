How to... talk about places

A: What’s your favourite place? — B: My favourite place is the beach.

A: Why do you like it? — B: Because it’s quiet and beautiful.

A: Is there a museum nearby? — B: Yes, there is.

A: Would you like to visit this place? — B: Yes, I would.

Grammar

1. Review — THIS / THAT / THESE / THOSE

This museum is fantastic.

That palace is very old.

These mountains are beautiful.

Those beaches are amazing.

2. Review — THERE IS / THERE ARE

There is a museum near my house.

There are some cafés in the city.

There isn’t a theatre here.

Are there any parks nearby?

3. Review — Adjectives

It’s a beautiful city.

The park is quiet.

The museum is interesting.

The market is busy.

The beach is amazing.

4. Asking about places

What’s your favourite place?

Why do you like it?

Is there a...?

Are there any...?

Would you like to visit...?
